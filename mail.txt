Hello everyone,


the student council is carrying out a small survey in order to gather basic information about the usage of public transport by students of Ulm University. Please take the time, it consists only of 15 questions and will take about 5 minutes.

  You can participate here:

    https://evaluation.uni-ulm.de/evasys/online

  Please use the following TAN:

     $TAN$
It is the goal of the survey to find out which means of public transport are used by the students to get to university of how many parking space is needed on the Oberer Eselsberg. We need this information to spot useful enhancements regarding public transport in Ulm and to strengthen our position in negotiations with e.g. the university's administration or the SWU (which runs Ulm's public transport system).

Further information (background, privacy, technology used for the survey...) and later also the results of the survey can be found here:
